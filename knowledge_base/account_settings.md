# Account Settings

## Changing email address
Go to Settings > Account > Personal Information > Email Address. The customer will need to verify the new email via a confirmation link before the change takes effect. Old email remains valid for login until the new one is confirmed.

## Feature requests
Feature requests should be acknowledged warmly, logged in the product feedback tracker with a tag for the relevant product area, and given an honest expectation that there's no guaranteed timeline. Never promise a specific release date for a feature request.

## Positive feedback / compliments
When a customer shares positive feedback about a specific past interaction, thank them, and where possible relay the compliment to the original agent or their manager. No further action needed beyond an acknowledgment.
