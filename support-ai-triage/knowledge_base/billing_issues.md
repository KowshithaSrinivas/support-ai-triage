# Billing & Refunds

## Duplicate charges
Duplicate charges in the same billing cycle are usually caused by a failed payment retry that succeeded on a second attempt without voiding the first. Verify in the billing system whether two distinct successful charges exist for the same subscription period. If confirmed, the duplicate should be refunded within 5-7 business days. No manager approval needed for refunds under $100; refunds above that require team lead sign-off.

## Unauthorized renewal after cancellation
If a customer claims they cancelled but were still charged:
1. Search support email archives and the cancellation log for a matching cancellation request.
2. If a valid cancellation request exists with a timestamp before the renewal charge, refund all charges from that date forward immediately — do not wait for escalation.
3. If no record of cancellation exists, explain the renewal policy calmly and offer to process the cancellation now, with a goodwill refund for the most recent cycle only, per company policy.
4. Any mention of legal action or regulatory complaints should be flagged for escalation to the support team lead, regardless of whether the claim appears valid.

## Refund processing time
Refunds typically take 5-7 business days to appear on the customer's statement, depending on their bank.
